About Me

I am an architecture student with a unique and extensive background in full-stack software development. For over 15 years, I designed and built logical systems in the digital world; today, I apply that same structured thinking and problem-solving discipline to the physical world.

My goal is to work at the intersection of design and technology, using tools like Rhino and Grasshopper to explore complex geometries and create efficient, elegant architectural solutions. This portfolio showcases my journey and my passion for creating meaningful spaces.
